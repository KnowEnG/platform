Account Information

Explorer Account

Be aware that because KnowEnG is providing the scalable computation for free, we have placed the following limitations on Explorer Accounts:

Data Retention

  • Uploaded spreadsheets older than 1 week are subject to deletion, after notification
  • Analysis results older than 1 week are subject to deletion, after notification

Uploads

  • Uploaded spreadsheets must be plain text files in .tsv format
  • The maximum size for any uploaded spreadsheet is 250MB
  • The cumulative total for all uploaded spreadsheets may not surpass 5GB

Computation

  • The total number of analyses concurrently queued or executing may not surpass 5
  • The number of bootstraps per analysis may not exceed 200
  • The total number of saved analysis results may not surpass 40

Premium Account

If you need additional resources and are interested in collaborating with the KnowEnG center, we can customize your account to meet your needs. Please contact knoweng@illinois.edu.
