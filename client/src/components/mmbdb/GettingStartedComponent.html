About
This prototype demonstrates unique features for studying microbiome
data and for comparing a single patient to groups of other patients.
Advanced analytics are used to identify important microbes that
differentiate cohorts for research objectives. Visualizations are
provided to compare data and to see relative abundances in the
context of the phylogenetic tree. The intention of this prototype is to
demonstrate potential use in both a research and clinical lab setting
where reports can be generated from these views. Funding provided by
the University of Illinois’s ARI Visual Analytics group and Mayo Clinic’s
CIM Microbiome group.
Demo Limitations
Presentation of this demo must be approved by the PIs. The following
functionality is referred to, but not implemented in this prototype:
  • ability to add new data and reprocess reads using Tornado
  • creation of new cohorts (sample of pre-built cohorts provided)
  • execution of new comparisons (samples provided)
  • processing of single patient analysis (sample provided)
  • database for rules/alerts
  • report generation
